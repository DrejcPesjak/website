About

Researcher-builder, currently focused on LLM interpretability.

I’m a PhD student and AI researcher at the Jožef Stefan Institute in Ljubljana. My current research is focused on mechanistic interpretability for transformer language models, especially sparse autoencoders, probing approaches, and intervention-style analyses.

Before moving deeper into LLM interpretability, I worked across applied machine learning, NLP, computer vision, MLOps, and large-scale data analysis. I like projects that start as strange ideas and end as working systems.

Currently

  • PhD: mechanistic interpretability for LLMs
  • Interests: SAEs, probing, causal interventions, representation analysis
  • Background: MSc Data Science, broad applied ML work
  • Location: Ljubljana, Slovenia
